I work primarily on the philosophy of mind, the philosophy of science, and epistemology. Within philosophy of mind, I am interested in the structure of our mental representations and computations. Within philosophy of science and epistemology, I am interested in the reliability of our statistical methods, especially ones that underlie computational modeling in contemporary data-driven science.
